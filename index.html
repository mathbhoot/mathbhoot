Where Math Meets The Unknown

The Protagonist

👨‍🔬

A mathematician lost in the shadows of an ancient library, seeking truth hidden in equations that defy logic. Their mind is their weapon, but fear... fear is their greatest enemy.

Once brilliant, now cursed to solve the riddles of darkness itself. Every number holds a secret. Every formula might be their salvation... or doom.

The Antagonist

👹

A presence born from forgotten calculations, a mathematical chaos that hungers for order. It speaks in numbers, thinks in algorithms, and feeds on human confusion.

Neither alive nor dead, it exists in the spaces between solutions. It has been waiting for a mind brilliant enough to understand its purpose... or foolish enough to challenge it.
