INTRODUCTION
---
Mathbhoot is an interactive educational universe that combines mathematics, storytelling, puzzles, exploration, and technology.
<img width="929" height="437" alt="home" src="images/image.png" />


Vision
---

Mathbhoot aims to transform mathematics from a collection of formulas into an immersive world of discovery.

Visitors explore a mysterious villa filled with mathematical secrets, hidden clues, puzzles, stories, and interconnected knowledge.

The project combines:
---

- Mathematics
- Storytelling
- Interactive puzzles
- Technology
- Visual exploration
- Educational content

Current Features

- Mathematical storytelling
- Puzzle-based learning
- Educational resources
- Expanding story universe

Future Roadmap

- Hidden mathematical mysteries
- Character-driven stories
- Puzzle progression system

- Advanced interactive experiences
